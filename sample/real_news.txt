The Ministry of Energy has announced a nationwide clean energy expansion program aimed at increasing the use of renewable energy sources over the next five years. The initiative includes investments in large-scale solar parks, wind energy projects, and improvements to the national electricity grid.

According to government officials, the program will provide financial incentives for businesses investing in renewable energy technologies and encourage households to adopt rooftop solar systems. The ministry stated that the initiative is expected to reduce greenhouse gas emissions while creating thousands of new jobs in engineering, construction, and maintenance.
Energy experts welcomed the announcement, noting that long-term investment in renewable energy infrastructure can improve energy security and reduce dependence on fossil fuels. The government confirmed that regular progress reports will be released to ensure transparency throughout the implementation of the project.
